About / Mission & History & Vision

🎯 Our Vision & Global Identity

The Technology and Society (TS) at SUNY Korea is an extension of the same department at Stony Brook University in New York, sharing the same mission, curriculum, and degrees. Our ultimate mission is to train you to make creative and innovative solutions for future society through global, interdisciplinary, and trans-disciplinary knowledge.

📌 Our Core Mission Aims

🌐 Why Our Global Environment Strategy Matters

• Cultural Diversity: An international basis of students with over 30 nationalities attending our campus (as of 2020), offering an environment vastly unique from other universities in Korea.

• SBU Prestige & Uniqueness: Benefit from a unique professional network and a degree from Stony Brook University. TS at SUNY Korea is the only such department in Korea and one of the few worldwide.

• Songdo Green Smart City: Direct links to unique communities and work opportunities in South Korea's premier tech hub, Songdo, paired with global scholarship support.

⏳ History of TS at SUNY Korea

The Department of Technology and Society is the oldest and largest of departments introduced to SUNY Korea by Stony Brook University.
