What is Technology & Society?

Technological Systems Management (TSM)

When you begin your academic journey with us, you will build the knowledge, skills and understanding of innovative technologies while examining the social, cultural and historical relevance of these technologies. You will have the opportunity to be engaged with world-class faculty who are passionate about their areas of expertise and dedicated to helping you succeed in and outside of the classroom.

Program Overview

Our program offers flexibility and diversity with a technically-based core curriculum, along with a required specialization in engineering fields (computer science, mechanical, electrical, civil) or natural sciences (mathematics, physics, chemistry). The department also offers a newly developed minor in industrial engineering.

Our foundation anchors on two major pillars: environmental sustainability and the potential of ICT to achieve it. We emphasize entrepreneurship, innovation, digital technologies, and project management to train present and future leaders.
